The Peaky Blinders

2013, tv series, seasons 1

Peaky Blinders is one of the most famous gangster families in modern television. Based on true events, the audience is transported to an industrial Birmingham that will turn (more than once) into a war zone, and the Peaky Blinders are always involved when the subject is violence and trouble.

Creator: Steven Knight

Cast: Murphy as Thomas Shelby, Helen McCrory as Polly Gray, Paul Anderson as Arthur Shelby, Finn Cole as Michael Gray, Sophie Rundle as Ada Shelby, Joe Cole as John Shelby, Tom Hardy as Alfie Solomons, Natasha O'Keeffe as Lizzie Stark

Genre: Drama, Crime

Author: Randy Otanez
